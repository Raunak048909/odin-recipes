Affogato

Affogato is an Italian coffee dessert made mainly with two ingredients: vanilla gelato or ice cream and hot espresso. While it comes from Italy, affogato has become a super popular after-dinner treat in the U.S. and beyond.

Affogato” literally translates to the word “drowned” in English to describe the act of pouring hot espresso over the ice cream. In essence, the ice cream is “drowned” in espresso.

Affogato is a simple yet delicious marriage of espresso and ice cream. Affogato is a unique dessert and quite possibly the most perfect pick-me-up on a hot afternoon or sweet finale after a lavish dinner. The creamy, sweet vanilla ice cream comes together with the pleasantly bitter bite of the espresso.

The magic of affogato is in its juxtaposition of hot and cold, sweet and bitter. This dynamic combination of sweet and creamy vanilla ice cream and hot, pleasantly bitter espresso makes it an irresistible treat. Ingridients

  • Espresso
  • Hot Water
  • Ice Cream or Gelato
  • fresh whipped cream
  • Savoiardi Ladyfinger

Steps

  1. Make the espresso shot.
  2. Scoop ice cream into a small glass.
  3. Pour the hot espresso shot over the ice cream.
  4. Top with whipped cream.
  5. Serve with a Savoiardi Ladyfinger cookie.
